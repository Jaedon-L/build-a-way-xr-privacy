About the Game

Build a Way XR is a physics-based puzzle game that transforms your room into a bridge-building playground. Design structures, test their strength, and take the wheel to see if your creations can carry you to the goal.

Your mission: get your car safely across. Build with platforms and supports, reinforce your design, then shift into drive and test it yourself. Every level brings tougher gaps, trickier terrain, and more interactions and obstacles for you to navigate.

Will your bridge stand strong, or will it collapse under pressure? Build, drive, and experience mixed reality like never before.
