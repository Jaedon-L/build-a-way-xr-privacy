About the Game

Build a Way XR is a physics-based puzzle game that transforms your room into a bridge-building playground. Design structures, test their strength, and take the wheel to see if your creations can carry you to the goal.

Your mission: get your car safely across. Place platforms and supports, reinforce your design, then shift into drive and test it yourself. Each level raises the challenge with wider gaps, interactive obstacles, and trickier terrain that put your engineering skills to the test.
